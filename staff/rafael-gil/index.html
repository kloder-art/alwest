Rafael Gil

Bio

Rafael Gil was born on May 22, 1913 in Madrid, Spain as Rafael Gil Álvarez. He was a director and writer, known for Teatro Apolo (1950), La calle sin sol (1948) and La guerra de Dios (1953). He died on July 10, 1986 in Madrid.
